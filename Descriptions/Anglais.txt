A couple of addictive games for you to kill time with; fight the ghosts and hunt them in Ghost Busters, fly your airplane to safety in Jump and avoid obstacles in Runner, all while trying to beat your high score, or the high score of your friends.
These three addictive games are a part of the Game Center we will release soon.
